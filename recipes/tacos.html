Tacos

Description

If you are having some friends over this coming weekend but you don't know what to give them for lunch, condsider this easy taco recipe. Hailing from the streets of mexico it is filled with culture and authentic flavor!

Follow the steps below to get your fiesta on this weekend and impress your guests with your cooking skills

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
    • 1 cup Thick and Chunky salsa
  • 10 taco shells
  • 1/2 head lettuce, shredded
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded chedder cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook beef for around 10 minutes over medium heat, occassionally stirring
  2. Stir salsa into beef. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium low. cook for 5 minutes, stirring occassionally.
  3. Heat taco shells as directed on package. Serve taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomato and cheese
