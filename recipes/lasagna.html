Lasagna

Description

Craving a bit of carbs in your diet? Perhaps you like pasta but you don't enjoy noodlie shaped things. In come lasgna. Sweeping in like a superhero to fill you craving and fight your fear of noodles

Below we have an easy to follow lasagna recipe for you to cook at home. This recipe will provide you with the neccessary steps to create a nice and creamy lasagna. Just like the Italians!s

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 pound ground beef

Steps:

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Melt the butter in a sauce pan
  3. Warm the milk in a seperate sauce pan. Don't let it boil
  4. Add the flour and stir to form a roux
  5. Slowly whisk in the warm milk, stirring well to get rid of any flour lumps. Season with kosher salt.
  6. Turn off the heat and reserve, covered.
